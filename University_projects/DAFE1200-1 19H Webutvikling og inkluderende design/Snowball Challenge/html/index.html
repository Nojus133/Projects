Bottle Up

With a mission to reduce single-use bottles and cups, we are introducing the BottleUp thermo bottle. With this bottle, you can fill up your drink of choice at selected retailers for a reduced price. This, for instance, includes coffee, tea, soda and sparkling water.

Join us, and help us keep single-use plastic out of landfills and waterways.

Learn more

Our highly functional bottle is designed with a triple-walled thermo technology, which keeps the temperature of your selected drink for several hours without affecting the temperature on the outside of the bottle. This technology also prevents condensation. With this bottle you can keep hot drinks warm for 12 hours, and cold drinks cool for 24 hours. Additionally, the mouth of the bottle is big enough to fit most ice cubes.

You can get the bottle in two different sizes, depending on your needs. The large bottle fits 750ml, the small one fits 500ml. Both sizes are available in a wide range of colours.

About us

BottleUp was founded in 2019, with the mission to let people buy and carry around their favourite drinks whilst reducing the use of single-use plastic bottles and cups. The result is a beautifully designed, highly functional, and eco-friendly bottle which will keep the temperature of your preferred drink just right throughout the day.

Imagine the millions of single-use cups and plastic bottles that are thrown away every day, after having served its use. Landfills, waterways and oceans are filled up with unnecessary singe-use plastic and materials. This have catastrophic consequences for our environment and animal life. Today, we are finally starting to see the consequences our actions have had on the environment, and because of that we see it as our duty to try to attack this global problem and reverse the trend of single use-plastics. We believe that if we put in just a little more effort into the making of every cup and bottle, these can last to be used a thousand of times rather than just one.

This is just the beginning of something big. We need YOU to help us make a difference. Get your bottle today, and help us save the environment.
